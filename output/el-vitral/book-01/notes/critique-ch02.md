## SHOULD fix
- **Foreshadowing anti-pattern — interpretation smuggled under a dismissal**
  (`references/prose-antipatterns.md` §"the over-explained clue"). The
  natalidad passage decodes its own subtext and *then* waves it off:
  «entendió que el cristal y el premio eran la misma mano, la que da con una
  cara para coger con la otra, y que la nuera de Brena había hecho bien todo lo
  que el mundo le había dicho que hiciera... y por hacerlo bien estaba ahora
  con la cara lisa». The thesis (`prisma-lee-drena`: medir=robar=premiar es
  despojo) is the very thing the seed envelope says to **show, not name** ("NO
  nombrar que mide=roba=caza: mostrarlo" — decisions-ch02). The draining scene
  already lands this physically; let the para. stop at the bare fact (Brena
  taking the vaciada by the arm) and cut the decoded conclusion. Test: after
  the plant a first-read reader should not be able to *state* the meaning —
  here they're handed it.
- **Same tic on the mother beat — decoded question + dismissal.** «le vino...
  la pregunta de por qué a su madre no la habría apartado nunca ningún
  cristal al lado bueno, por qué a su madre... escondida en la casa como
  escondía Bruno los gestos, nunca le había oído contar que la hubieran
  leído. Pero la pregunta no tenía dónde agarrarse... y Bruno la dejó irse».
  This nudges `la-madre-era-blanca` (SHADOW-TRUTH, reveal cap **hidden**,
  "apenas sensed; confirma Libro II") whose *only* carrier is
  `telar-hilo-verge` — a seed **not active this chapter**. Routing the linaje
  mystery through an unseeded carrier, in a chapter whose envelope is the
  three convoy plants, pre-loads a Libro-II reveal. Keep the cold-in-the-belly
  *sensation* (that leaks nothing) but drop the spelled-out "why was she never
  read / she hid like I hide" reasoning — the explicit link is the leak.
- **POV reasons past the dose-clean mandate on `prisma-lee-drena`.** Bruno
  works out the harvest logic in full ("lo que querían era lo que la gente
  traía dentro... el modo de que viniera mucho era premiar que se hiciera
  mucho"). The chapter is the *plant* (dose 2); the cribado/convoy-jovenes
  payoff is Saúl's cap 3 and Libros II-III. Bruno grasping the whole
  premiar-natalidad economy is more cognition than a marrón peón who "no
  entiende la física" (decisions-ch02) should carry. Trim to the felt horror,
  leave the economy for cap 3's POV.

## CONSIDER
- **Word count under the stated minimum** (7705 vs 8000). Script reads
  in_range on tolerance, but the chapter has room for one more texture
  dwelling — e.g. the *grises ya leídos* standing apart (the beat sheet's
  "diferencia de luz entre los que aún brillan y los grises" is only lightly
  touched). Adding it would also re-balance the heavy interior codas.
- **Brena's household scale vs the ch1 anchor.** decisions-ch02 describes her
  ch1 house as "dos voces, un murmullo cálido"; here it's "la casa más llena,
  con los hijos y los nietos y la nuera." Not a hard canon line (ch1 isn't a
  loaded contradiction), but the jump from two voices to a full multigen
  household is worth a continuity glance.
- **Transition-out is buried.** The beat sheet's hand-off (convoy continues
  toward the green village → cap 3) lands at the carts-leaving para., then two
  more scenes follow (dusk, the jergón). The hook still exists but the chapter
  doesn't end on it. Fine as is; flag only if cap 3's seam feels cold.

## What works
- The draining of Brena's nuera is the chapter's spine and it is excellent:
  «no se vio salir nada de la mujer... dejó muy despacio de tener la cosa que
  tiene la gente que está al sol». `el-polvo-gris` and `prisma-lee-drena`
  planted by *showing the after*, no denuncia — exactly the brief. The "cara
  lisa, una casa con las ventanas puestas y nadie dentro" image is
  load-bearing, not decorative.
- `lee-marron` planted clean by others' disinterest: «de aquel no había nada
  que sacar» + Bruno's hidden flood of relief. The misread is cultivated by
  honest detail (he really does read flat), never by a narrator lie. Textbook.
- The grey water poured into the brocal-less well — the seed lands as a
  physical fact (the well of all of Vega Parda) and the horror is in how
  *normal* it is; no underlining.
- Opening on the convoy's *sound* coming up the road (not waking/battle/
  prophecy), measured through Bruno's body and the threshing — strong
  slow-immersion. Cost never in numbers; no blanco frío / Blanco×Negro /
  lineage leak. Shadow held.
- Subtext of "estarse quieto" as the only thing Vega Parda knows to say to the
  irreversible — Brena mouthing it to her nuera, Bruno saying it to himself —
  carries theme without stating it.
