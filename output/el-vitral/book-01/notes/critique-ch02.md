## MUST fix

- **[CANON — caste pyramid]** — *"el crío de los Sales había salido **amarillo bajo**, una alegría pequeña para una familia pobre, un color que daba sitio"*
  → `canon/factions.md`: the pyramid is **Blanco > Magenta > Amarillo/Ciano (altas casas) > Rojo/Azul/Verde (primarios) > Marrón > Negro**, and "movilidad imposible por mérito; sólo por depredación." A peasant child reading **amarillo** is *alta casa* — that is not "una alegría pequeña," it would be a sensation that disrupts the whole village (a marrón family producing a high noble color). It also undercuts the established bleakness. Fix: make the Sales child a **primario** (verde, rojo, or azul) — a modest, real step up from marrón that genuinely "da sitio" without breaking the pyramid (Senén himself is a verde peasant, so a primario peasant child is canon-clean). Or leave it marrón.

- **[STYLE — the one explicit ban: aphoristic narrator]** — three instances of the book's *single* forbidden target (style.md §"The one forbidden target"; flagged in `voice.md` as the recurring reflex to watch from ch 1). Each is a universal maxim in timeless present / a closing epigram:
  1. *"no el miedo al lobo, que **es un miedo viejo y manso porque al lobo se le conoce**, sino el miedo a una pisada en la nieve que no es de ningún animal que se haya visto nunca"* — generalizing maxim. Keep the snow-footprint image; cut the "es un miedo viejo y manso porque…" gloss, anchor it to what the *plaza* feels.
  2. *"la otra mitad, la del **animal viejo que llevamos dentro**"* — the first-person-plural universal ("que llevamos dentro") is the narrator climbing up to generalize. Anchor it to la Brena's body (the arms that pull the child back), not to a shared human nature.
  3. **Closing epigram** — *"No sabía que de aquel camino no volvería igual, ni que no volvería; **eso lo saben los que cuentan las historias, no los que las viven.**"* — this is exactly the "closing-paragraph epigram where the narrator climbs up to philosophise" style.md names. The beat-sheet transition only asks for "No sabe que no volverá igual"; render that through Bruno's not-knowing (he's busy with the cazo) and delete the meta-narratorial gloss.

## SHOULD fix

- **[Word count / dwelling]** 7498 is under the 8000 floor and well short of the ~10k midpoint write-chapter targets. The **recaudación plaza** beat is the thinnest — the registro, the casta-marks, the weight of the señor's eyes are summarized more than dwelt in. Add one 300–500w texture dwelling here (the prism/Lectura dread of the two mothers in the crowd; or the physical ritual of the count — names read aloud, bodies stepping forward to be confirmed against a ledger). This also buys back room lost by trimming the aphorisms.

- **[Seed telegraphed — `recuerdo-que-se-apaga` echo]** — *"lo otro —que algo se le hubiera ido, otra vez, sin volver, **en el mismo momento exacto en que la viga se paraba en el aire**— lo otro no tenía dónde meterse."* The beat sheet asks for a *"recuerdo echo leve"* and shadow Truth 3 reserves the reader's *understanding* of the memory-cost mechanism for **ch 13**. Welding the memory-loss causally to the magic use ("en el mismo momento exacto") hands the reader the ch-13 link now. Keep Bruno reaching for warmth and finding the smooth hole, keep him blaming the cold — but let the *juxtaposition* do the foreshadowing; drop the narrator's explicit simultaneity. (The cold, not the memory, is the reader's diagnostic cue in this chapter.)

- **[Thesis-adjacent gloss]** — *"esa misma cosa que lo había mantenido a salvo toda la vida… fuera también lo que ahora, exactamente por eso, lo entregaba sin defensa al primero que sí quisiera algo de él."* The narrator explains the irony of "marrón = invisible = unprotected" rather than letting it land. Trim to the fact (a marrón doesn't constar, so no one will miss him — the forastero says as much) and cut the explanatory frame. (Also contains the rule-of-three *"no faltarle a nadie, no constar, no importar"* — drop to two beats.)

- **[Opening leans gnomic]** — *"Doce días son pocos para juntar lo que no se tiene, y en Vega Parda los gastaron casi enteros en esconder."* Not cliché (good — not a waking/battle open), but abstract/sententious where ch 1 opened on pure sensation (the smell of tin). Consider grounding the first line in the *sound* of a village hiding (digging where no one digs, jergones being unpicked) before the gnomic turn.

## CONSIDER

As written it reads oblique enough; keep an eye on it.

Using it on the mentor is a nice anticipatory echo (ch 4 beat sheet wants exactly that preview), but it slightly dilutes Vela's signature. Fine to keep; just be aware it's spent here.

A half-line earlier showing the horses nervous in the crowded cobertizo would harden it.

## What works

- **The seed plants land clean.** `bruno-no-se-calienta` is excellently set up: the rojo guard forcing the sacks and *sweating* ("forzar quema. Hasta a esos"), the whole plaza bracing for a cooked Blanco Falso, and then the frozen, vaho-breathing boy in his circle of empty ground. The reader gets the anomaly viscerally without a word of explanation. `borradores-del-mentor` (the libreta, the cramped paño-escrito calligraphy, the classifying signs that rhyme with Senén's círculo-con-raya) is planted with real menace under genuine warmth.
- **The `poste-flojo` payoff is maximally earned** — propped "hoy no" in ch 1, echoed in the twelve-days telar visit, paid off on la Tona. "Hoy no" finally becoming "hoy sí" is the chapter's quiet spine.
- **Sensory/colour mandate is met every scene** — magenta livery "pesaba como el cielo antes de tronar," the rojo's heat at three steps, the forastero's "frío de agua de pozo," the chapel ventanuco gone black at the end. The synesthetic premise is felt, not named.
- **Mauca's farewell** ("Aguanta" / "Lo de siempre") and the **Senén/piedra** beat carry their grief in objects and gesture, exactly per the anti-cursi rule — no one names the feeling.
- **Bruno's voice holds** — insights stay as things "guardó sin querer" / "no lo pensó con esas palabras," the narration carrying the texture while Bruno stays austere.
